It’s been six months of us… and when I think about it, it’s not just time it’s everything we’ve been through together. We’ve had our share of good days and hard days. Moments that felt easy, and moments that didn’t. Times when we understood each other perfectly, and times when everything felt a little confusing. But through all of it… you stayed. And that means more to me than I can ever fully explain. Merci d’être restée.
I know I’m not perfect. I make mistakes, I overthink, I don’t always get things right. And somewhere deep down, I always had this fear that one day it might be too much for you. But you never left. There’s something I once read—“Love isn’t about finding someone perfect, it’s about seeing an imperfect person perfectly.” And when I think about us, it feels true. Because even with all my flaws, you chose me… and you kept choosing me. Je te choisis, encore et encore.
Somewhere along the way, you’ve taught me so many things—about patience, about understanding, about what it really means to care. I don’t say this enough, but thank you for that. Tu es devenue ma paix.
You’re not just someone I love… you’ve truly been a charm in my life. You made things feel lighter, calmer, and more meaningful just by being there. Six months might seem small, but to me, it’s something real—something we built together. I don’t know what the future holds, but I do know this—I want you in it. No matter what comes, I’ll keep choosing you. Mon cœur est à toi.
Because you’ve become my safe place. Thank you for staying. For understanding me. For being you. Happy 6 months, love. Jusqu’à la fin… je te choisis.
— yours Shawn
